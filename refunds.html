Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Cancellation Process

Passengers can cancel their booked tickets online up to 2 hours before the scheduled bus departure. To cancel your ticket, please contact our support desk at support@gujaratbusseva.in with your PNR number and registered mobile details.

2. Refund Rules & Deduction Slab

Refund amounts are calculated based on the timing of your cancellation request relative to the scheduled departure time:

Cancellation more than 24 hours before departure 90% Refund
Cancellation between 12 to 24 hours before departure 50% Refund
Cancellation less than 12 hours before departure No Refund

3. Processing Time

Once a cancellation is initiated, the refund will be credited back to the original source account (UPI, Card, or Wallet) used during Razorpay checkout. The refund process generally takes 5 to 7 business days to reflect in your bank statement, depending on your bank's processing cycles.
