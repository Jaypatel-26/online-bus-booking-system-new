Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when booking a ticket, such as your name, email address, mobile number, and payment information. This information is necessary for confirming your bus seats and communicating booking details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to process your bus ticket bookings, send PNR confirmations, and provide customer support. Payment information is securely processed by our payment gateway partner (Razorpay) and is never stored on our local servers.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with external third parties except as necessary to process your transactions or comply with legal obligations. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and security measures to protect your data.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how your personal data is handled, please reach out to us at support@gujaratbusseva.in.
